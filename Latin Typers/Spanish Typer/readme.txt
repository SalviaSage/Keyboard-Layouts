Spanish Typer;

The acute accent (acento) can be typed by pressing control+space.
The diaeresis (diéresis) can be typed by pressing control+shift+space.
The thin space can be typed by pressing control+alt+shift+space.

The inverted exclamation mark and the inverted question mark is at the 6 key.
The ordinal indicators can be typed by pressing the 7 and 8 keys.
The ordinal indicators can also be typed with Ctrl+Numpad0 and Ctrl+NumpadDot.

Inverted exclamation mark is at the '!' key.
Inverted question mark is at the shift+'@' key.

The "ü" is at the '{' key.
The "ñ" is at the '}' key.
The "ç" is at the '\' key. Only used in Old Spanish.

However, the backslash character cannot be typed.
