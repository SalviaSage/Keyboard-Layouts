Spanish Typer;

The acute accent (acento) can be typed by pressing control+space.
The diaeresis (diéresis) can be typed by pressing control+shift+space.

The accented letters are at 1 to 5 keys.
The ordinal indicators can be typed by pressing the 6 and 7 keys.
The inverted exclamation mark and the inverted question mark is at the 9 and 0 keys.

The "ü" is at the '{' key.
The "ñ" is at the '}' key.

Inverted exclamation mark is at the '\' key.
Inverted question mark is at the shift+'\' key.
However, the backslash cannot be typed.
