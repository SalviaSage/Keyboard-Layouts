Spanish Typer;

The acute accent (acento) can be typed by pressing the backslash (\) key. Also by pressing ctrl+spacebar.
The diaeresis (diéresis) can be typed by pressing shift+backslash key. Also by pressing shift+ctrl+spacebar.
The thin space can be typed by pressing control+alt+shift+space.

The inverted exclamation mark is at the shift+1 key.
The inverted question mark is at the shift+2 key.

The ordinal indicators can be typed by pressing the 7 and 8 keys.
The ordinal indicators can also be typed with Ctrl+Numpad0 and Ctrl+NumpadDot.

The "ñ" is at the '[' key.
The "ç" is at the ']' key. Only used in Old Spanish.

However, the backslash character cannot be typed.
