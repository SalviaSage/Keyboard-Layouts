Pinyin Typer;

Fully types Pinyin and some extra diacritics.
Switch between Pinyin Latin alphabet and English Latin alphabet by pressing the scroll lock key.

Type the tonal diacritics by first typing the vowel and pressing the top row number keys 1 to 4 and the extra diacritics by pressing 7 to 0.

Press the (5) and (6) keys to type the superscript nasal which is sometimes used.

The letter (ê) is at the ({) key.
The letter (ü) is at the (}) key.
The letter (ṳ) is at the (\) key.
