Braille Typer (English);
Types the letters, numbers and punctuation marks in Braille.
Cannot be read by a blind person, but can be read and seen visually.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Braille

"Capital Follows" (⠠) is located at shift+tab.
"Number Follows" (⠼) is located at the backtick.
"Accented Letter Follows" (⠈) is located at shift+backtick.
