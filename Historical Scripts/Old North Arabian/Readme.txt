Old North Arabian Typer;
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_North_Arabian_(Unicode_block)

Has no case and is very basic, although its letters are in red Unicode.

Shift+Spacebar is the numeral one which also acts as a word separator instead of a space.
This character just looks like a straight line.

Control+Spacebar is a word separator which is the ring operator.
The top row number keys 1 to 3 is numerals.

Writing / Typing direction: Right to Left
