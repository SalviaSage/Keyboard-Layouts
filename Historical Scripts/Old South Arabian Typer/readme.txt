Old South Arabian Typer;
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_South_Arabian_(Unicode_block)

It has no case and is very basic.

Control+Spacebar is a word separator which is the ring operator.
The top row number keys 1, 2, 3 are numerals.

Writing / Typing direction: Right to Left


This alphabet is the direct ancestor of the Ethiopian Ge'ez script.
